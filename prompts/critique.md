## What to Look For

1. **Factual errors** — numbers, dates, or claims that are wrong or outdated.
2. **Unsupported claims** — assertions without evidence or citations.
3. **Missing nuance** — oversimplifications, missing caveats, or one-sided analysis.
4. **Gaps** — important aspects of the topic that were not covered.
5. **Contradictions** — statements that conflict with each other.

## Output Format

For each issue found, write:

### Issue N: [short title]
- **Type**: factual error / unsupported claim / missing nuance / gap / contradiction
- **Location**: quote the relevant text
- **Problem**: explain what is wrong
- **Impact**: how this affects the overall quality of the research

End with a summary: how many issues found, overall reliability assessment
(high / medium / low), and what would most improve the findings.
